GREEN SPINE CONCEPT

Irrespective to any other Housing Projects having square or round shape Parks here in MIVIDA Pakistan the Green Areas have been spread out all around the Project like Green Ribbons known as Green Spines. Public walkways and jogging tracks have been designed throughout Green Spines and the geographical placement of Green Spines makes the Residents capable to reach any part of the Project while walking at the Green areas.

BOTANICAL GARDENS

Botanical gardens or botanic gardens are generally well-tended parks displaying a wide range of plants labeled with their botanical names. At MIVIDA Pakistan we are going to offer a state of the art botanical garden with futuristic approach. Botanic Gardens act as an educational center for students (School, College and University) and also the interested persons. The beauty of the Botanic Garden and its charming environment gives pleasure and enjoyment to the general public.

URBAN JUNGLES

Urban Jungle refers to a lush oasis located among concrete and buildings, creating an escape from big city life by bringing the beauty of a green environment into your home, in other interiors, and in public spaces. It is the most promising way of connecting people, plants and food, within an inspirational settings within home or in a living vicinity.
